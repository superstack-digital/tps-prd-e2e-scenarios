AME-001
Create Americano tournament with minimum 4 players
Happy Path · Setup

Preconditions

  • Organizer is logged in and has created an event
  • Event has at least 1 court
  • At least 4 participants registered to the event
  • No active tournament on this event

Steps

  1. Organizer navigates to event → Tournament tab → "Create Tournament"
  2. Selects tournament type: Americano
  3. Selects exactly 4 players from participant list
  4. Selects 1 court
  5. Sets maxPoints to 16
  6. Taps "Generate Tournament"

Assertions

  • Tournament is created with status pending
  • Round 1 contains exactly 1 match (4 players → 1 court)
  • Match has team1Player1, team1Player2, team2Player1, team2Player2 all populated
  • Each of the 4 players appears in exactly one team slot
  • Match score is null, status is pending
  • Match maxPoints = 16
  • Standings table shows 4 entries, all with 0 points

Edge Cases

  • 3 players selected → creation fails with "Minimum 4 players required" error
  • 0 courts selected → creation fails with court validation error
  • 8 players, 2 courts → 2 matches created in round 1, all 8 players play
AME-002
Submit score and verify standings update
Happy Path · Scoring

Preconditions

  • Americano tournament active, round 1 has 1 match (4 players)
  • Match is in pending state

Steps

  1. Organizer opens round 1 match
  2. Enters Team 1 score: 16, Team 2 score: 9
  3. Taps "Submit Score"
  4. Navigates to Standings tab

Assertions

  • Match status changes to completed
  • Team 1 players each have wins = 1, points = 2 (or configured win value)
  • Team 2 players each have losses = 1, points = 0
  • Team 1 players have pointsFor = 16, pointsAgainst = 9
  • Team 2 players have pointsFor = 9, pointsAgainst = 16
  • Team 1 players ranked above Team 2 players in standings
  • Round 1 shows as completed in UI

Edge Cases

  • Tied score (8–8) → both teams receive ties = 1, equal standings points
  • Score exceeds maxPoints (e.g., 20–15 when maxPoints=16) → validation error shown
  • Non-organizer submits score → rejected with permission error
AME-003
Round 2 uses different pairings from round 1
Happy Path · Rotation

Preconditions

  • Americano with 8 players, 2 courts
  • Round 1 all matches completed

Steps

  1. Complete all round 1 scores
  2. System auto-generates round 2
  3. Compare round 2 match player assignments to round 1

Assertions

  • Round 2 is generated automatically after all round 1 matches completed
  • Round 2 still contains 2 matches (all 8 players participate)
  • At least one court has a different 4-player combination than round 1
  • No player appears in more than one match in round 2
  • All 8 players are assigned to exactly one match in round 2

Edge Cases

  • With exactly 4 players, round 2 has same 4 players but potentially different team splits
  • Pairing is random — system does NOT guarantee different partners (no history check for Americano)

AME-005
Final standings tiebreaker by point differential
Edge Case · Standings

MEX-003
Standings-based pairing: rank 1+3 vs rank 2+4
Happy Path · Pairing Logic

Preconditions

  • Mexicano with 4 players: A (rank 1), B (rank 2), C (rank 3), D (rank 4)
  • Round 1 complete, standings established

Steps

  1. Complete round 1 so clear ranking exists: A(1st) B(2nd) C(3rd) D(4th)
  2. Observe round 2 pairing

Assertions

  • Round 2: Team A+C versus Team B+D (ranks 1+3 vs 2+4)
  • Not: A+B vs C+D (which would be rank 1+2 vs 3+4)
  • With 8 players + 2 courts: Court 1 has ranks 1+3 vs 2+4, Court 2 has ranks 5+7 vs 6+8
⚠ Known Bug (RC-1): Standings fetched with .order('points') instead of .order('rank'). If two players have equal points but different ranks (due to tiebreaker), pairing may be incorrect. Fix: use .order('rank', ascending: true).

MEX-006
Partner history check prevents same partner in consecutive rounds
Edge Case · Partner Rotation

Preconditions

  • Round 2 being generated

Steps

  1. Complete round 1 with A+C vs B+D
  2. Observe round 2 pairing

Assertions

RR-001
Create Round Robin: all matches generated upfront
Happy Path · Setup

Preconditions

  • Event with 4 teams registered
  • Feature flag miniTournament enabled

Steps

  1. Select tournament type: Round Robin
  2. Add 4 teams
  3. Configure: single round robin, Win=3pts, Draw=1pt, Loss=0pts
  4. Generate tournament

Assertions

  • Total matches = 4×(4-1)/2 = 6 matches generated immediately
  • Each team plays exactly 3 matches (vs every other team once)
  • All 6 matches show as pending
  • No team plays itself
